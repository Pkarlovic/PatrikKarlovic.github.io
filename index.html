About me:


Hi everyone! My name is Patrik Karlovic.

I am excited about data and the insights it can provide into all aspects of our world. From looking at home energy use to global carbon emissions, utilizing data enables us to craft solutions for challenges both known and unknown. Exploring a multitude of topics through data is what drew me to this field, especially climate and energy analysis, sports analytics, NLP, and data generation using neural networks.
